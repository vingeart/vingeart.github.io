## 参数功能 ##

|Bright Color|亮色|
|Dark Color|暗色|
|Normal Map|法线|
|Normal Intensity|法线强度|
|Specular|高光|
|Light Dirction|灯光方向|
|Light Warpping|灯光包装|
|Fresnel Range|菲涅尔范围|
|Center Intensity|中间亮度|
|Edge Intensity|边缘亮度|
|Parallax Map|视差贴图|
|Parallax Depth|视差深度|
|Parallax Intensity|视差强度|
